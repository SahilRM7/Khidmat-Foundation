Together We Can Make a Difference

Khidmat Foundation is dedicated to helping the poor, needy, and underprivileged sections of society with love and care.

About Us

Khidmat Foundation is a non-profit organization that works tirelessly to support the poor and needy. Our mission is to provide food, education, medical assistance, and shelter to those in need. We believe in humanity, compassion, and selfless service.

Our Work

Food Distribution

We organize food drives to feed the hungry and distribute rations to needy families.

75th Amrit Mahotsav

Helping children from poor families with books, tuition, and school supplies.

Healthcare camps

Providing healthcare support and medicines to the underprivileged.

We celebrate Shiv Jayanti with cultural programs and community gatherings to honor Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Ambedkar Jayanti

We celebrate Ambedkar Jayanti with cultural programs and community gatherings.
